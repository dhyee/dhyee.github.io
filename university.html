UNIVERSITY OF LEICESTER REDESIGN

FALL 2014 | UX Researcher & Designer

The University of Leicester’s website is a website designed to help inform prospective students and families about what their University is about and how to apply. However, the current website was very much cluttered and required many clicks and digging to find out how to apply as a prospective student. We, as a team of 3, evaluated the usability and information architecture of the University of Leicester’s website to redesign it.

I took on the role of coding a majority of the HTML/CSS of the home page, with the exception of the Events and Footer section and coding a majority of the secondary pages. I also wireframed the secondary page layout and creating the graphics for the footer background as well as most of the header backgrounds.

Type: Web Design, Branding, Graphic Design
Tools: Adobe Photoshop CS5, Axure, HTML/CSS, Javascript
