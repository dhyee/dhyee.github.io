I worked on redesigning the University of Leicester's website in a team of 3 in the course Usability & Information Architecture.

We evaluated the usability and information architecture of the original university website, changing aspects that were lacking while keeping those that we found useful.

I took on the role of coding a majority of the HTML/CSS of the home page, with the exception of the Events and Footer section and coding a majority of the secondary pages. I also wireframed the secondary page layout and creating the graphics for the footer background as well as most of the header backgrounds.

Type: Web Design, Branding, Graphic Design
Tools: Adobe Photoshop CS5, Axure, HTML/CSS, Javascript
