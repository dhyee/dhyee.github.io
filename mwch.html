I worked on redesigning UC San Diego's on-campus coffee vendor Muir Woods Coffee House's website in a team of 3. We worked closely with our client, the Director of Muir Woods Coffee House, in order to gauge and meet his needs as well as interviewing users to meet user needs.

I took on the role of coding a majority of the HTML/SCSS. I also created the graphics for the menu page and took and editted all the photos (with the exception of the Muiristas photos) on the page.

Type: Web Development, Graphic Design, Web Design, Branding

Tools: Adobe Photoshop CS5, Axure, HTML/CSS, SASS, Javascript
