FUSION XV

WINTER 2015 | Graphic Designer




Fusion is the biggest student run dance competition in UCSD. This year being the fifteenth Fusion, I was on the graphics committee to help make promotional graphics in order to help bring awareness and promote the dance competition to UCSD students on campus and off campus.

I created various graphics to advertise the upcoming Fusion XV using Adobe Photoshop CS5. I created graphics including but not limited to: Posters, Flyers, TV Advertisements, Cover Photos, etc.

Type: Graphic Design
Tools: Adobe Photoshop CS5
