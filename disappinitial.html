DISAPP Initial Design

←

→









I worked on a restaurant application project with a team of 11 people. I was the UI specialist so I was in charge of the look and feel of the application.

Initially, I wanted to go for a dark/chic and flat looking restaurant application that had a pop of color. I made several drafts on both paper and on photoshop. Depending on how many buttons we wanted on the home page, there would be different layouts and color schemes.

Each button and the chef logo on top of DISAPP were all drawn and made on Adobe Photoshop CS5. Fonts and colors were hand picked by yours truly.

Type: User Interface, Android Application
Tools: Adobe Photoshop CS5, Java, XML
