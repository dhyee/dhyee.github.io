Who I Am —

A UI/UX Designer currently based in Los Angeles designing web and mobile apps.

I graduated from the University of California San Diego with a degree in Computer Science specializing in HCI. Because of my background, I'm a designer that can not only sympathize well users, but with developers as well. I’m genuinely interested in how people interact with various products in their lives and what I can do to improve those experiences.

When I'm not working, I like to take artsy fartsy photos, try out new food, draw ridiculous things in Photoshop, and travel the world on my free time.
