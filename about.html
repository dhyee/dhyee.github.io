Hello, again!


I'm an aspiring UX Designer and recent graduate from the University of California San Diego majoring in Computer Science with a passion for seamless user experiences. I have a lot of practical experience in Human and Computer Interaction because that's what I'm excited to do. I'm a designer that can sympathize well with the programming side of things.

I want to create things that people enjoy using by utilizing my design skills and programming knowledge. My goal is to bridge the gap between designers and programmers to create a more visually aesthetic but functionally efficient world.

When I'm not designing or programming, I enjoy photography, art, eating new food, and traveling on my free time.
